Optimizing Engineer Well-being Through Innovation

Our research focuses on the intricate relationship between workload management and the well-being of software engineers. By delving into advanced technologies such as facial analysis and machine learning, we strive to identify and mitigate stress levels effectively. Our mission is to pave the way for a more sustainable and fulfilling work experience for all engineers, ensuring optimal productivity and mental health.
